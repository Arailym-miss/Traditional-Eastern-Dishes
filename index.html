Traditional Eastern Dishes and Delicious Desserts

Plov

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Beef or lamb
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable oil
  • Spices

Preparation:

Meat is fried with onions and carrots in oil. Rice and spices are added, then everything is cooked together with water until the rice becomes soft. Plov is served hot and is one of the most popular eastern dishes.

Lagman

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spices

Preparation:

Noodles are boiled separately. Beef and vegetables are fried with spices to make a rich sauce. The noodles are served with the hot meat and vegetable sauce on top.

Baklava

Ingredients:

  • Phyllo dough
  • Honey
  • Walnuts or pistachios
  • Butter
  • Sugar syrup

Preparation:

Thin layers of dough are filled with nuts and butter. After baking, sweet honey syrup is poured on top. Baklava is a famous sweet dessert in many eastern countries.

Halva

Ingredients:

  • Sesame paste
  • Sugar
  • Honey
  • Nuts

Preparation:

Sesame paste is mixed with sugar and honey. Nuts are added and the mixture is cooled until it becomes firm. Then it is cut into small sweet pieces.

Shawarma

Ingredients:

  • Chicken or beef
  • Pita bread
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Garlic sauce
  • Lettuce

Preparation:

The meat is grilled and sliced into thin pieces. Fresh vegetables and sauce are placed in pita bread together with the meat. Then it is wrapped and served warm.

Samsa

Ingredients:

  • Puff pastry
  • Minced lamb or beef
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preparation:

Minced meat is mixed with chopped onions and spices. The filling is placed inside pastry dough and baked in the oven until the samsa becomes golden and crispy.

Turkish Delight

Ingredients:

  • Sugar
  • Cornstarch
  • Water
  • Rose water
  • Pistachios

Preparation:

Sugar and water are cooked to create a thick syrup. Cornstarch is added to make the mixture soft and chewy. Nuts and rose water are mixed in, and the dessert is cut into small cubes.
